Recipe for Cinnamon Rolls

Ingrediants

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup of Warm Milk.
  • 1/4 cup granluated sugar.
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups of bread flour.

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter.

Instructions

  • Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar and eggs.
  • Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  • Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  • Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  • Transfer the dough has doubled in size, rollit into a large rectangle.
  • Melt the butter for the filng and mix in the sugar and Cinnamon.
